Bandhav Veluri

I'm a Research Scientist at Sesame AI working on conversational AI. Previously, I spent a couple of years at Meta Superintelligence Labs where I worked on one of the first full-duplex spoken language models based on auto-regressive LLMs. I graduated with a PhD in Computer Science at the University of Washington advised by Prof. Shyam Gollakota and Prof. Michael Taylor. I completed my Bachelor's degree at the Indian Institute of Technology Roorkee.
